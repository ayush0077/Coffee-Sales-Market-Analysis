# Coffee Sales Market Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes coffee sales data to identify **high-potential cities for coffee shop expansion**. Using SQL and Python-based data analysis, the project evaluates sales performance, customer activity, and market demand across multiple cities.

The analysis focuses on transforming raw sales data into **actionable business insights** that can support strategic expansion decisions.

---

## Dataset

The dataset consists of multiple relational tables representing different aspects of the business:

* **city** – city information including population and estimated rent
* **customers** – customer details and city association
* **products** – coffee product catalog and pricing
* **sales** – transaction records including product purchases, sales amount, and ratings

These datasets were integrated to perform **multi-table relational analysis**.

---

## Project Objectives

* Estimate the **number of coffee consumers in each city**
* Analyze **sales performance across cities**
* Identify **top-selling coffee products**
* Evaluate **average customer spending by city**
* Compare **sales performance with rental costs**
* Recommend **optimal locations for new coffee shop expansion**

### Database Design

### Sales Analysis

* Calculated **total revenue across cities**
* Identified **top-performing products and cities**

### Customer Analysis

* Evaluated **customer distribution and activity by city**

### Market Potential Analysis

Combined multiple metrics to determine expansion potential:

* Sales revenue
* Estimated coffee consumers
* Number of active customers
* Rental cost per city

---

## Key Insights

The analysis identified the **top 3 cities for coffee shop expansion**:

### Pune

* High total revenue
* Low average rent per customer
* Strong sales performance

### Delhi

* Highest estimated coffee consumer base
* Large number of customers

### Jaipur

* Strong customer activity
* Competitive rental costs

These cities demonstrate **strong market potential for business expansion**.
